About Us

Hack4Futo

Hack4Futo was born out of a desire to encourage innovation in the student tech ecosystem. We want students to know they can build great products and be confident in their abilities while doing so.

Hack4Futo 1.0

The maiden Edition, held in June 2023. Won by Alpha-Developers for building an innovative skill transfer product.

Hack4Futo 2.0

The second edition, held in march 2024. Won by Team Ectoware for building a game on the blockchain for Solana ecosystem.
